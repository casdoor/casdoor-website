Casdoor is based on OAuth. Tokens are users' OAuth token.

- `Owner`
- `Name`
- `CreatedTime`
- `Application`
- `Organization`
- `User`
- `Code`
- `AccessToken`
- `ExpireIn` Tokens will expire in hours
- `Scope` Scope of authorization
- `TokenType` E.g. type `Bear`
